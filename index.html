IBM Cloud

Product Team

  • Role: Product Designer & Fascilitator
  • Type: Team Project
  • Duration: 11 Months (Ongoing)
The Question

How do you create a unified experience
for all IBM Cloud Users?

IBM is bringing Bluemix and SoftLayer, its two largest cloud platforms, together. They provide capabilities that enable enterprises to reinvent the way they develop software, but the experience of using both platforms is very difficult and disjointed. I have participated in several major projects related to this effort: First, creating a design system to unify the look and feel of the platforms; Second, fascilitating workshops to promote Agile Development and IBM Design Thinking; Third, resdesigning user management and designing migration paths to create a unified experience for all IBM Cloud users.

Design System

Create a consistent look and feel by developing
a library of styles, components and interactions.

As part of a guild of designers and front end developers, I had the opportunity to establish UI patterns for color, typography, iconography and interaction. In addition to brand considerations, we also worked to ensure that all patterns met accessibility standards.

One of my contributions was advocating for a consistent type scale. A type scale adds consistency to the typography without being overly perscriptive to the designer. I created assets that designers could use in Sketch to reference the type scale in their designs. I also worked with the front end developers to implement that type scale as Sass variables to streamline implementation.
